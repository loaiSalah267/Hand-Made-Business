our project

Our idea is characterized by handicrafts from palm remnants and we add with it the leather, whether natural or artificial, and it goes through several steps to obtain the product

  • After the date harvest season, we take the bunch and peel it.
  • We take out the lameen from it and separate it from each other in the form of strings.
  • We put lambs and water to facilitate their formation.
  • We put it on the loom with a specific craft, and then mix it with the leather and make the product to be shaped from it.
